						***Digit of Life***

*asks the user her/his birthday (in the format YYYYMMDD, or YYYYDDMM, or MMDDYYYY - actually, the order of the digits doesn't matter)
*outputs the Digit of Life for the date




bday = input("enter your birthday in YYYY MM DD: ")

bday = bday.replace(" ","")

# print(type(bday))
total = 0
for each in bday:
    each = int(each)
    total += each
    #ammend
if total > 10:
    total_2 = 0
    total = str(total)
    for i in total:
        i = int(i)
        total_2 += i
    print(total_2)
else:
    print(total)
